Last updated: January 1, 2025 · Bilimxon platform

1. Data We Collect

  • Name, username, email address

2. How We Use Data

  • Account creation and authentication
  • Tracking and personalizing the learning process

3. Third Party Sharing

We do not sell or rent user data to third parties. Data may only be shared in legal requests (court orders) or with trusted technical service providers.

Bilimxon uses necessary cookies for system operation (session, language settings). See our Cookie Policy for details.

5. Data Security

Industry-standard encryption is used. Passwords are stored with bcrypt algorithm. We never disclose personal information to anyone.

6. Your Rights

  • Delete your account (Settings → Account → Delete)
  • Request data export
